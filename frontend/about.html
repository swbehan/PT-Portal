What Is PT Portal?

PT Portal is a web-based application designed specifically for physical therapy clinics and their patients. It brings together everything a clinic needs to manage patient care into one clean, easy to use platform. Physical therapists can build and assign weekly workout plans, attach exercise videos and instructions, and track how their patients are recovering through daily session reviews. Patients get a clear view of their exercises, a simple way to log how each session went, and the ability to book appointments without coordinating over email. Referring doctors can check in on patient milestones without needing to make a phone call. PT Portal keeps everyone connected and informed throughout the entire recovery process

Why PT Portal over another platform?

Most existing solutions are either expensive enterprise software that clinics can barely afford, or generic fitness apps that were never built with physical therapy in mind. PT Portal was designed from the ground up for the PT workflow. It is role based, meaning every user sees exactly what they need and nothing they don't. It is lightweight and accessible from any browser without downloading anything. And unlike a shared Google Doc or an email thread, it keeps all communication, progress tracking, and workout management in one organized place. PT Portal is not trying to be everything for everyone. It does one thing well, and that is helping patients recover faster through better communication with their care team
