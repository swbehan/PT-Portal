Why Us?

Most physical therapy offices still rely on Google Docs and scattered email chains to manage patient care. PT Portal was built to fix that. We created a single platform where therapists can assign and track workout plans, patients can stay on top of their recovery, and referring doctors can monitor progress without being in the weeds. Everything is in one place, communication is streamlined, and nothing gets lost. A cleaner, more connected way to get you back to what you love.
